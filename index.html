Pierre Bizeul

Email: pbizeul@imj-prg.fr

I am a postdoctoral researcher in mathematics at the Technion Institute under the supervision of Emanuel Milman. I graduated from Sorbonnes Universités in 2023 where I did my PhD under the supervision of Dario Cordero-Erausquin and Joseph Lehec.

I am interested in high-dimensional phenomena at the intersection of geometry, probability, and analysis, with a particular focus on problems involving convexity.
